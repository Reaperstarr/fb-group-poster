English

Irishka Group Master by SBS («the Extension», «we», «us») is distributed by Slimme Brug Solutions. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is handled, where it is stored, and whether and with whom it is shared when you use the Extension and related services (licence validation, payments).

1. Data collection — what we collect

  • Content you create locally: post text, spintax, images, Facebook group names/URLs, schedules, and preferences.
  • Facebook interaction data: page content visible on facebook.com to automate posting. We do not collect your Facebook password.
  • Clipboard (optional): only when you use copy/paste features.
  • Pro licence data: licence key, device/installation identifier, app version, IP address and request metadata sent to our licence server.
  • Purchase data: email and billing identifiers via Stripe (no full card numbers in the Extension).
  • Optional AI spintax (BYOK): text sent from your browser to Groq and/or OpenAI with your API key.
  • Optional Telegram: bot token, chat ID, and status messages you configure.
  • Technical data: posting state and errors stored locally.

We do not sell personal data or collect Facebook login credentials.

2. Data handling — how we use information

  • Publish to Facebook groups you select.
  • Save drafts, groups, images, and settings.
  • Validate Pro licences.
  • Process subscriptions and deliver licence keys.
  • Send optional Telegram alerts.
  • Generate spintax when you request it with your own API key.
  • Comply with law and provide support.

3. Data storage — where and how long

  • On your device: chrome.storage.local, chrome.storage.session, IndexedDB. Until you delete data or uninstall.
  • Licence server: keys, device IDs, email/plan while active and as legally required.
  • Stripe, Groq/OpenAI, Telegram: per their policies when you use those features.

4. Data sharing — who receives information

  • We do not share drafts or group lists with third parties in normal use.
  • Meta/Facebook when you post.
  • Our licence server for validation.
  • Stripe — Stripe Privacy.
  • Groq/OpenAI (optional) — their privacy policies.
  • Telegram (optional) — Telegram Privacy.
  • Legal disclosure when required by law.

We do not sell or rent personal information.

5. Cookies and similar technologies

The Extension uses browser storage APIs. Facebook and Stripe may use cookies on their websites.

6. Security

Reasonable measures for server data. Local data depends on your device and Chrome profile security.

7. Your rights

Under GDPR and similar laws: access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, portability. Contact requests@slimmebrug.solutions.

8. International transfers

Providers may process data outside your country with applicable safeguards.

9. Children

Not directed at children under 16.

10. Changes

We may update this policy; check the date above.

11. Contact

requests@slimmebrug.solutions
